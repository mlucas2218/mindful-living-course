Welcome to the course website!

This course was taught at Rice University, Houston, TX, USA by Melissa Lucas in the Fall 2013 semester. Thank you to all of the students and guests who made it a success!

Although the course has ended, you are welcome to explore the resources posted throughout this site.

Course Description

This student-taught course explores the effects of modern-day food, pharmaceutical drugs, and toxins on health from a young adult perspective. We will go beyond conventional nutrition to critically examine naturalistic approaches that use diet and lifestyle choices to cure disease and live optimally, all on a college student's budget.

Open Door Policy

While only the 15 students who are registered can receive course credit, anyone is more than welcome to come sit in on any or all classes. This course is meant to be a collaborative learning experience where we can learn from and bounce ideas off each other, so feel free to come listen, learn, and join in discussions (or even bring a friend) for any class session that seems interesting to you!
